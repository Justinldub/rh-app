About Us

We are a team dedicated to simplify the process of getting workers within the domestic field into their work place across South Africa by compiling all necessarily documents that a potential employer may need.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple, we locate the employer as well as a helper close by to help the employer with which ever domestic sort of job is required by the employer. We do so taking into consideration the fact that both security for both employer and employee is set in place. Therefore we use experts advice and sophisticated credit as well as criminal record tools available to us to do deep background check. And lastly an interview conducted within 3 parties to further analize both employee and employer to in sure that both employer and employee are satisfied with each other prior to the commencement of service.

Our Vision

Our vision is to eliminate the large cluster of unemployed community within South Africa by leveraging the technological equipment to bring potential employers and employees close to each other in relational mutual agreement for eradicating unemployment rate in the country.

Our ability to bring outstanding results to our clients.
